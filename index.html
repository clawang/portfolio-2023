About Me

I'm Claire (王宇静), a bicoastal developer & designer based in SF and NYC. I work full-time as a software engineer at Google, working on music at YouTube, and I also do freelance web work for music artists.

When I'm not doing that, I'm usually curating playlists, vintage shopping, cafe-hopping, or traveling.

I graduated from NYU in 2021 with a B.A. in Computer Science and a minor in Web Programming and Applications.
